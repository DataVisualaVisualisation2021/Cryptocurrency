1. What is cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a currency in digital form, which is enhanced with cryptographic security. This cryptographic security is considered almost impossible to bypass. Cryptocurrency is also decentralized, due to the fact that it can be distributed across different computing entities, which are not controlled by governents or other authorities. One more important term related to cryptocurrency is the one of "Blockchain". Blockchain is the platform where cryptographic transactions are executed.

Reference: https://www.investopedia.com/terms/c/cryptocurrency.asp

3. Why are cryptocurrencies so popular?

There are multiple reasons for cryptocurrencies being famous. Some of these popularity factors are:

  • Very little fees are involved.
  • Govermental authorities are not impacting cryptocurrencies.
  • Cryptocurrency users can be benefited financially, making money.
  • Cryptocurrency is secure.

Reference: https://worldfinancialreview.com/seven-reasons-why-cryptocurrency-is-so-popular/
